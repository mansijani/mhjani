HOW DOES IT WORK?

Tablets. Laptops. Phones. If it has Wi-Fi, it can use Karma.

With Karma, you pay for data as you go, and take it with you on your 4G mobile hotspot.
Post pics to Facebook. Catch up on the news. Video chat with your kids. You know what to do.

Why is it different?

The more you share, the more FREE data you earn.

Your Karma hotspot has an open Wi-Fi signal. Anyone around can connect to it.
When they do you'll both receive 100MB of free data. It's Social Bandwidth.
